About Me

I'm a CS and Maker teacher at the American School of Barcelona, where I teach IB Computer Science, Game Development, and run the Makerspace. My classes cover everything from Python and Flask to laser cutters, 3D printers, and CNC machines. I believe the best CS students are the ones who like to poke around and break things — curiosity is the engine. My classroom motto: Low Floor, Wide Walls, High Ceiling.
